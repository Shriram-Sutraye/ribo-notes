> 3. If something breaks within 1 year, they'll fix it
> 
> If the contractor disappears mid-project, the surety pays 50-100% of the contract to hire someone else to finish!

> [!TIP]

> The contractor hires electricians and buys lumber. If the contractor goes bankrupt and can't pay them:
> - The electricians still get their wages
> - The lumber yard still gets paid for materials
> 
> The SURETY pays the workers and suppliers. This stops construction projects from having unpaid workers/suppliers.

| Protects the project AND the people working on it |

> [!TIP]
> **Plain Dumb Down Version:**
> Performance bond protects the PROJECT OWNER.
> L&M bond protects the WORKERS and SUPPLIERS.
