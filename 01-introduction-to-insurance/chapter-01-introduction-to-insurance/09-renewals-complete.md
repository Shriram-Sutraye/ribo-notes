### SABS Changes & Renewals (July 1, 2026)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **For existing policies renewing AFTER July 1, 2026:**
> - Insurers MUST renew with the SAME coverages and limits as previous term
> - UNLESS the consumer explicitly agrees to changes IN WRITING
> - Brokers must inform consumers of the lowest available rate
> - TAC (Take-All-Comers) rule still applies

| Before July 1, 2026 | After July 1, 2026 |
|---------------------|-------------------|
| Standard SABS benefits | Modular system - more client choice |
| Bundled coverages | Some benefits become optional |
