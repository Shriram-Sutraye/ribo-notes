> Insurance companies need to know:
> - What are you shipping? (Electronics vs. bricks)
> - Where does it come from? (Safe country vs. risky route)
> - Is it fragile? (Glass vs. steel)
> - Does it need special care? (Temperature-controlled?)
> - What ports will it stop at? (More stops = more risk)
> - How is it travelling? (Boat vs. plane vs. truck)
> 
> All of this affects your premium and coverage terms.
