## 3ï¸âƒ£ Net Income

**Definition:** The profit or revenue stream that a business generates. If operations stop, net income stops.

### Why It Matters:
> If a fire destroys a restaurant, the **building** is a tangible property loss. But the **lost profits** while closed for repairs? That's a **net income** exposure.

### Components of Net Income Loss:

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Lost Revenue** | Sales you would have made |
| **Continuing Expenses** | Rent, salaries, utilities you still pay |
| **Extra Expenses** | Costs to keep operating (temporary location) |

### Example:
> **Sarah's Coffee Shop (Revisited)**
> 
> Fire closes the shop for 6 weeks.
> 
> | Loss Type | Amount |
> |-----------|--------|
> | Normal monthly profit | $15,000 Ã— 1.5 months = $22,500 |
> | Rent still due | $5,000 |
> | Employee wages (to keep them) | $8,000 |
> | **Total Net Income Loss** | **$35,500** |

### Insurance Solution:
- **Business Interruption Insurance** covers net income loss
- Also called "Loss of Business Income" coverage

## 4ï¸âƒ£ Key Personnel

**Definition:** People who are essential to a business's success. If they're gone, the business suffers.

### Who Qualifies as Key Personnel?

| Role | Why They're Key |
|------|-----------------|
| **Founder/CEO** | Vision, leadership, relationships |
| **Top Salesperson** | Generates majority of revenue |
| **Chief Scientist** | Holds critical knowledge |
| **Key Partner** | Contributes capital or expertise |
| **Specialized Technician** | Only person who can do certain tasks |

### Real-Life Scenario:
> **Small Law Firm**
> 
> The firm has 3 partners:
> - Partner A brings in 80% of clients
> - Partners B and C do the legal work
> 
> If **Partner A dies**, the firm loses most of its new business. Even though no physical property was damaged, the **value of the firm drops significantly**.

### Insurance Solutions:
| Coverage | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| **Key Person Insurance** | Pays business if key person dies/disabled |
| **Buy-Sell Agreement** | Funded by life insurance to buy out deceased partner's share |
| **Disability Income** | Replaces income if key person is disabled |
